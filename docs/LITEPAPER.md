## Abstract

Billions are spent annually on hair care, yet millions of people with natural hair still rely on guesswork, unverified influencer advice, and fragmented routines that lead to frustration and wasted resources. **CronoCapilar** is a decentralized social network built on [Stellar](https://stellar.org) that transforms daily hair care actions into verifiable, on-chain records called **Proof of Care**. By anchoring real self-care behavior to a transparent ledger, CronoCapilar creates a trust layer where reputation is earned through consistent action — not followers, ads, or algorithms.

---

## 1. The Problem

Natural hair care is one of the most personal and culturally significant routines in the world. Yet the experience for most people follows a painful, recurring cycle:

- **Invisible routines.** There is no universal system to track what treatments work, when they were done, or how hair responded over time. Memory is the only record, and memory fades.
- **No trustworthy history.** When visiting a salon or seeking advice online, users cannot prove what they have done. Professionals work without context, and recommendations start from zero every time.
- **Influencer noise.** Social media is flooded with product endorsements driven by sponsorship, not results. There is no way to distinguish genuine care knowledge from paid content.
- **Professional blind spots.** Hair care professionals have no access to a client's historical data. Every consultation begins with subjective guessing.

The result is a multi-billion-dollar market built on **trust deficits** — where the people who care most about their hair have the fewest tools to make informed decisions.

---

## 2. The Solution

CronoCapilar is not a tracking app. It is a **decentralized social network for hair care** where every action of self-care becomes a building block of identity and reputation.

Users sign up to CronoCapilar, create a hair profile, and begin registering daily treatments — Hydration (H), Nutrition (N), or Reconstruction (R) — along with significant events like Big Chop, haircuts, or coloring. Behind the scenes, the app uses a Stellar-compatible wallet (e.g., Freighter) to record each registration on-chain as a **Proof of Care** entry.

Over time, these records form a verifiable hair care passport: an immutable timeline that belongs entirely to the user. This passport becomes the foundation for:

- **Community trust** — Users earn reputation through real actions, not vanity metrics
- **Professional context** — Stylists and trichologists can view a client's verified history
- **Informed product choices** — Real data replaces influencer hype

CronoCapilar is free for every user, always.

## 4. Proof of Care Protocol

**Proof of Care (PoC)** is CronoCapilar's core mechanism. Unlike proof-of-work or proof-of-stake, Proof of Care is non-financial and non-speculative. It measures one thing: **consistent acts of self-care, verified on-chain.**

Every time a user registers a treatment, validates another user's experience, or maintains a streak of daily care, they generate Proof of Care. These proofs feed into a reputation score that reflects genuine dedication — not capital, not popularity.

Key properties of Proof of Care:

- **Non-transferable** — Reputation cannot be bought, sold, or delegated
- **Time-weighted** — Recent and sustained activity matters more than one-time bursts
- **Decay-enabled** — Inactivity gradually reduces reputation, ensuring scores reflect current behavior
- **Community-validated** — Peer endorsements amplify Proof of Care weight

## 5. Soulbound Passport

Each user's accumulated Proof of Care is represented by a **Soulbound Passport** — a non-transferable token (SBT) that evolves visually as the user progresses through their hair care journey.

The Passport includes four evolving badge tiers:

| Badge | Meaning |
|:------|:--------|
| **Bloom** | Awakening — The user has started their care journey and established initial consistency |
| **Rise** | Growth — Sustained streaks, diverse treatments, and early community engagement |
| **Crown** | Authority — Deep consistency, validated by peers, recognized as a trusted voice |
| **Elder** | Legacy — Long-term dedication, mentorship activity, and community leadership |

The Soulbound Passport serves as a portable, verifiable credential. It can be presented to professionals, shared in the community feed, or used to unlock marketplace features — all without revealing private hair data unless the user chooses to.

## 6. Reputation Engine

CronoCapilar's Reputation Engine converts Proof of Care into a dynamic score that determines a user's visibility and authority within the network.

The score is influenced by:

- **Treatment frequency** — Consistent daily check-ins contribute steadily
- **Streak length** — Longer unbroken streaks carry increasing weight
- **Treatment diversity** — Balanced H/N/R routines score higher than repetitive patterns
- **Community validation** — Endorsements from other reputable users amplify score
- **Event milestones** — Significant events (Big Chop, long-term transitions) contribute bonus reputation

**Decay mechanism:** To prevent stale profiles from occupying authority positions, reputation decays gradually during periods of inactivity. This ensures the community feed and rankings always reflect currently active and engaged users.

## 7. Revenue Model

CronoCapilar is and will always be **free for end users**. Revenue is generated through three complementary pillars that leverage the trust layer created by Proof of Care:

### Marketplace
A curated product marketplace where brands and sellers are ranked by community Proof of Care data. Sellers pay a commission on transactions. Products validated by high-reputation users gain visibility — creating a virtuous cycle where quality rises to the top.

### B2B Intelligence
Aggregated, anonymized insights from public on-chain data are packaged into intelligence reports for hair care brands. These reports reveal treatment trends, product sentiment, and regional care patterns — enabling brands to develop products grounded in real community behavior.

### Pro Tools
A subscription tier for hair care professionals (stylists, trichologists, salon owners) offering access to client care timelines (with user consent), appointment context, and professional reputation building within the network.
